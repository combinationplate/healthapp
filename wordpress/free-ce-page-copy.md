## Setup (5 minutes)
1. Install the updated pulse-connect plugin zip (Plugins → Add New → Upload → "Replace current with uploaded").
2. WP Admin → Pages → Add New. Title: **Free Sponsored CE Courses**. Slug: **free-ce**.
3. Paste the copy below into the page, then add a **Shortcode block** where marked with `[pulse_ce_request_form]`.
4. Publish. Then set the SEO title/meta (below) in your SEO plugin if you have one.

## PAGE COPY (paste from here down)

# Free Sponsored CE Courses

**Quality education shouldn't come out of your pocket.** Local hospice, home health, and rehab
organizations sponsor our nationally accredited CE courses for nurses, social workers, case
managers, and other healthcare professionals in their communities — at no cost to you.

## How it works

**1. Tell us what you need.** Fill out the short request below — your discipline, where you are,
and what course topic or hours you're looking for.

**2. A local sponsor picks it up.** Healthcare partners in your area sponsor CE requests through
our partner platform, Pulse. Most requests are matched within a few days.

**3. Your course arrives by email.** One click and you're in the course — no account setup, no
checkout, no credit card. Same accredited H.I.S. Cornerstone courses, same certificate.

[SHORTCODE BLOCK HERE: pulse_ce_request_form]

## Frequently asked questions

**Is this really free?**
Yes. The sponsoring organization covers the course cost. You'll never be asked for payment
information.

**Are the courses accredited?**
Every course is the same nationally accredited H.I.S. Cornerstone catalog you see in our store —
approved for RNs, Social Workers, Case Managers, and more, across all 50 states. Your certificate
is identical to a purchased course.

**What's the catch?**
There isn't one. Sponsors are local healthcare organizations building relationships with
professionals in their community. Your course email will note who sponsored it.

**What if no sponsor is available in my area?**
Requests stay open until matched, and our team monitors every request. Most areas are covered
within days.
